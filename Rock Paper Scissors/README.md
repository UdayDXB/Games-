# 🪨 Rock Paper Scissors: Battle Edition
A classic Python implementation of the world's most famous game. Test your luck and strategy against the computer!

## 🎮 How to Play
1. **Open** the `Rock-Paper-Scissors` folder.
2. **Run** `rps.py` in your terminal or VS Code.
3. **Input** your choice: `rock`, `paper`, or `scissors`.
4. **Result:** The computer will reveal its choice and the winner is declared!

## 🚀 Features
- **Instant Result:** Immediate feedback on wins, losses, or draws.
- **Looping Gameplay:** Play as many rounds as you want.
- **Score Tracker:** Keep track of your winning streak! (Optional: If you added this to your code).

## 🛠️ Built With
- **Python 3**
- **Random Module:** Used to generate the computer's unpredictable moves.
